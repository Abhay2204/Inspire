Origin...

Inspire Engineering Services is founded by Ratheesh N in 2018 in Kerala, our company began its operations at the Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) , initially focusing on the fabrication and erection of industrial structures and the maintenance of crucial equipment. With a strong emphasis on delivering quality work within stringent timelines, we quickly earned a reputation for reliability and precision in the power sector.
 

Adani Power Limited..

In 2020, we transitioned to Adani Power Limited , expanding our capabilities to manage larger-scale projects. This shift allowed us to diversify our service portfolio, which now includes the erection of sophisticated equipment, repair and maintenance of boiler components, and the upkeep of rotating machinery.
 Under the leadership of Ratheesh N, who brings over 17 years of industry experience, we continue to uphold our commitment to delivering high-quality solutions, safety-first practices, and timely project completion, making us a trusted partner in the industrial sector.
